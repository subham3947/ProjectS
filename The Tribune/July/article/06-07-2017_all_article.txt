Tata Motors today said it has cut prices of its commercial vehicles by up to 8.2% to pass on the GST benefit to customers. We are offering a price reduction in commercial vehicles (cargo) in the range of 0.3% to 4.21% and in commercial vehicles (passenger transportation) in the range of 0.6% to 8.2%,Tata Motors Head of Commercial Vehicle Business unit Girish Wagh said. Tata Motors is the largest commercial vehicle player in the country.
